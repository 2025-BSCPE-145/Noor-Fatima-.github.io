Balancing Assignments and Quizzes

📅 June 5, 2026 • ⏱️ 4 min read • 🏷️ Student Life

One of the biggest challenges I faced during this semester was balancing assignments and quizzes. At the beginning of the semester, everything seemed manageable because there were only a few tasks to complete.

However, as the semester progressed, the workload gradually increased and managing different subjects became much more difficult.

📚 The Overwhelming Week

There were weeks when we had assignments from Digital Logic Design, Database Systems, and Object-Oriented Programming at the same time. Along with these assignments, quizzes were also scheduled during the same period.

It felt as if every subject required immediate attention, and there was never enough time to finish everything comfortably.

"I often found myself moving from one task to another throughout the day. After completing one assignment, I had to prepare for a quiz. After studying for the quiz, another assignment deadline would appear."

💡 Learning Beyond Subjects

During these busy weeks, I realized that university life is not only about learning technical subjects. It is also about learning how to manage responsibilities.

Every assignment and every quiz taught me something about discipline, planning, and time management.

⏰ The Power of Starting Early

One lesson that became very clear to me was the importance of starting work early. Whenever I delayed an assignment, I ended up feeling stressed when another task appeared.

On the other hand, completing tasks earlier gave me more time to prepare for quizzes and revise important concepts.

⚡ My Balancing Strategies

  • ✅ Start early – Begin assignments as soon as they're given
  • ✅ Create a schedule – Plan your week in advance
  • ✅ Set priorities – Identify urgent vs. important tasks
  • ✅ Avoid procrastination – Don't wait until the last moment
  • ✅ Stay consistent – Small daily efforts add up

👨‍🏫 Advice from Dr. Bilal Ahmad

Dr. Bilal Ahmad often encouraged us to become consistent learners and avoid postponing our work. At first, I thought that working under pressure was normal for university students.

However, after experiencing several busy weeks, I understood why consistency is so important. Small efforts made every day can prevent a great deal of stress later.

🎯 The Value of Priorities

Another important lesson I learned was the value of setting priorities. Some tasks are more urgent than others, and identifying those priorities can make work much easier.

Creating a schedule and planning my day helped me stay more organized and reduced unnecessary pressure.

💪 Developing Valuable Skills

Despite the difficulties, balancing assignments and quizzes helped me develop valuable skills. I improved my ability to work under pressure, manage my time, and stay committed to my responsibilities.

These skills will not only help me in future semesters but also in my professional career as an engineer.

🚀 Moving Forward

Looking back, I realize that every challenging week taught me an important lesson. University life can become busy and demanding, but with proper planning and discipline, it is possible to manage different responsibilities effectively.

As I continue my journey in Computer Engineering, I want to improve my time management skills and become more organized. The experience of balancing assignments and quizzes has taught me that success does not come from working at the last moment. It comes from consistent effort, planning, and the willingness to learn from every challenge. 🎯
